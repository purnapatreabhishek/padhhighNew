Why Padhhigh ?

OUR STUDENTS ARE OUR UTMOST PRIORITY

We here at PadHIGH cover various subjects like- MATHEMATICS, SCIENCE, VEDIC MATHS. We take these subjects very seriously and take monthly tests of the same. Other than these subjects we also cover theoretical subjects like English And SST in form of tests only. With all the understanding comes a stage where you don't actually understand everything and simountensly doubts arise, we acknowledge the doubts and try to solve them for you and make learning easier for you.

ROLE AS MENTOR

We try to make a good relationship with parents and children both, we try to keep a friendly yet inspiring environment around us. We encourage children to work hard, we believe that it's not only parents responsibility to look after the children but also the mentor's job. Considering us as mentors we take full responsibility to keep the parents updated with how well the students are doing academically, their monthly tests reviews, their performance graph, everything. The parents have full rights to be updated how well the students are doing in their academics.

DAILY REVISION AND REFLECTION OF CONCEPTS

We need to keep a check on whether the students are studying properly or not, and to keep a check on that we ask the children to send in their notes as soon as they get over with the class.The teachers then check up the notes and guide the students further.This also helps the teacher know how well the students are understanding the topics

Weekly TESTS

There are monthly tests of 50 marks at the end of the month which will comprise all the chapters which he learnt in that particular month.The report card will be shared with the parent the next day.The report card will consist of the results of all the 3 tests and one end of month test. It will be notified where the child is lacking in that particular subject and where he needs to work on right now.

LIVE SESSION AND DISCUSSION

The mentor will indulge with parents once a week over video calls so that he/she can know where the child is facing problems.The mentor will give topics to the students and they have to speak on that in english.Through this child’s public speaking and thought clarity will improve.
